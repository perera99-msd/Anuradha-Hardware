Our Story

Founded in 1995, Anuradha Hardware has grown from a small neighborhood hardware store to one of Sri Lanka's leading suppliers of quality hardware and building materials. What started as a modest family business has now expanded to serve contractors, builders, and DIY enthusiasts across the country.

Our commitment to quality products, competitive pricing, and exceptional customer service has been the cornerstone of our success. We take pride in helping our customers bring their construction and home improvement projects to life.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality hardware products and building materials at competitive prices, backed by knowledgeable service and support that helps our customers successfully complete their projects.

Our Values

  • Quality: We source only the best products from trusted manufacturers
  • Integrity: We conduct business with honesty and transparency
  • Expertise: Our team has the knowledge to help you make the right choices
  • Community: We're committed to supporting local builders and contractors
  • Innovation: We continuously expand our product range to meet evolving needs
