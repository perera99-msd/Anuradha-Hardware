Product Overview

The Professional Hammer 16oz is the perfect combination of power and precision. Designed for demanding applications, this hammer features a forged steel head that's hardened for maximum durability and impact resistance.

The fiberglass handle is engineered to absorb shock and reduce vibration by up to 50% compared to traditional hammers. This innovative design minimizes user fatigue during extended use, allowing you to work longer and more efficiently.

Key Features

  • Magnetic nail starter for one-handed nailing
  • Textured, non-slip grip for maximum control
  • Anti-vibration technology reduces fatigue
  • Polished striking face prevents damage to work surface
  • Lifetime warranty against defects

Ideal for framing, construction, demolition, and general carpentry work, this professional-grade hammer is built to withstand the toughest job site conditions while providing exceptional performance.
