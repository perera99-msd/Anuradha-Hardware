Product Description

The 18V Cordless Drill Set is designed for professional contractors and serious DIYers who demand power and durability. The drill features a high-performance motor that delivers 450 in.lbs. of torque and up to 1,500 RPM for fast drilling and screwdriving.

The ergonomic design features a comfortable grip and balanced body to reduce user fatigue during extended use. The 24-position clutch provides precise control for consistent screwdriving, while the 2-speed gearbox allows you to select the optimal speed for your application.

This set includes two 18V lithium-ion batteries that provide long runtime and quick charging, a charger, a carrying case, and a double-ended bit tip.
